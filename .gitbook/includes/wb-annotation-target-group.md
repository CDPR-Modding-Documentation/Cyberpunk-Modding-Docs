---
title: 'WB annotation : target group'
---

The <mark style="color:purple;">target group</mark> <i class="fa-square-plus" style="color:purple;">:square-plus:</i> is the group used as destination for any newly added item.\
\
<i class="fa-check" style="color:$success;">:check:</i> <mark style="color:$success;">This applies to :</mark>&#x20;

* Items spawned from the [Spawn New tab](../../modding-guides/world-editing/object-spawner/ui-tabs-explained/tab-spawn-new.md).
* Favorites/prefab loaded from the [Favorites sub-tab](../../modding-guides/world-editing/object-spawner/ui-tabs-explained/tab-favorites-and-prefabs.md).
* Repeated spawn using the 'R' shortcut while in [3D editor](../../modding-guides/world-editing/object-spawner/3-d-editor-mode.md).
* Items generated in <mark style="color:purple;">brush paint mode</mark> <i class="fa-paintbrush" style="color:purple;">:paintbrush:</i>.

<i class="fa-xmark" style="color:$danger;">:xmark:</i> <mark style="color:$danger;">This does not apply to :</mark>&#x20;

* Duplicated element.
* Groups loaded from the [Saved tab](../../modding-guides/world-editing/object-spawner/ui-tabs-explained/tab-saved.md).
